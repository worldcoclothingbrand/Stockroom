# Stockroom

Stockroom is a local inventory app for tracking products, creating Code 128 barcodes, printing labels, and recording sales that automatically reduce inventory.

## Open the app

Double-click `Launch Stockroom.bat`, or open `index.html` in your browser.

## What it does

- Add and edit products with SKU, category, cost, price, stock, reorder level, notes, and barcode.
- Generate scannable barcode SVGs automatically.
- Select products and print product labels.
- Ring up sales from the checkout tab.
- Reduce stock after each completed sale.
- Track retail inventory value, cost basis, projected margin, low stock, and sales totals.
- Export all product and sales data as JSON.

Your data is stored locally in this browser with `localStorage`.
